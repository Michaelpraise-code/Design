About US
The Mission that drive our God-given mandate
Sustaining an experiential knowledge of God and walking in the fullness of our eternal ordinations.
We believe that knowing God is more than intellectual assent; it's about encountering Him personally and experiencing His presence in our daily lives. We encourage a vibrant faith that is nurtured through prayer, study & practice of the Word, fellowship, and service.

Discipleship Programs

Equipping the Sons of God through our Apostolic College
We provide a structured pathway for growth, beginning with foundational spiritual disciplines and progressing to specialized ministerial training.
Our Apostolic College offers a comprehensive suite of discipleship programs designed to cultivate spiritual maturity, leadership excellence, and effective ministry. Through foundational training, mentorship, and practical experience, we empower individuals to live out their God-given purpose.

Partnership

Your generosity empowers our mission to win souls and impact lives both locally and globally.
We are united by a common desire to make a tangible difference in the lives of others, reflecting the love and compassion of our Lord and saviour Jesus Christ.
‍By partnering together, we can amplify our impact and reach more people than we could alone.
‍We offer various opportunities for you to get involved, whether through financial support, volunteering your time and skills, or offering prayerful support.
